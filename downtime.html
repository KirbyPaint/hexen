Downtime

These moves can only be done when resting. It requires a safe location.

When Downtime is started, spend 1 food per character in the group. If you cannot, your party members who didn't eat gains the Tag: Hungry. If already Hungry gain the Tag: Starving. If already starving, the party member dies.

Each character gets 6 points to allocate. You can only use a Downtime move once per downtime.

Downtime Moves

Patch Wounds - 4

You can heal 1 minor wound. Expend an Herb to heal an additional minor wound. Can be used on other characters.

Scrounge for food - 2

Roll+Clever. On a 10+ you find 1 Food. On a 7-9 you find 1 Food and pick 1 from the options below:

  • The food is spoiled. (The Food causes Nausea when consumed)
  • You have to fight for it. (Take a minor wound)
  • You stole it from someone or something in need. (Take 5 Mind Damage)

On a miss you find 1 food, but prepare for the worst.

Cope - 4

You calm yourself from the horrors of the world. If it is the first time you use this ability, decide what that looks like for the character. It's never pretty.

Gain 10 Mind. You may expend Drugs to increase this to 20 Mind.

Conversation - 2

Talk to another party member about something that has happened in the past. If you don't have anything the DM can provide a topic. The player gains +1 Connection with that character (Max +3).

Attune - 2

You seek enlightenment from the gods. Gain 1 Rev Token each battle until the next downtime. Roll+Occult. On a hit, and the DM tells you something new and interesting about the current situation, and might ask you a question or two; answer them. On a 10+, the DM gives you good detail. On a 7-9, the DM gives you an impression. If you already know all there is to know, the DM will tell you that. On a miss, be prepared to be interrogated by the DM.

Mend Armor - 4

During downtime you can repair damaged armor. Choose a piece of armor. That armor gains 1 use per Scrap spent.

Bonding - 0

If you have a +3 Connection with someone you may tell them a secret about your character. A real non-bullshit secret. If you do, they can increase one of your Stats by 1 (Max +3).
