Appendix C: Abilities

Fighting

Taunt At the start of the round you may force an enemy to attack you if possible. Elite enemies can only be taunted once per encounter.
Defensive Stance In this stance when you guard allies enemies hit you with -1 intensity. You can only be in one stance at a time.
En Garde You may always first strike.
Orders CHARGE Sacrifice your next turn. Every ally you choose can attack again at the end of this turn.
Melee Proficiency Gain +1 when attacking with a melee weapon. Can use advanced tags on melee weapons.
Wicked Blows Status effects you apply with Melee attacks have increase potency
Killer Instinct You roll two dice for initiative and take the higher result.
Fast Stance In this stance you get an additional action at the end of your turn. You can only be in one stance at a time.
Boxing Unarmed attacks gain the tags Quick and lose the tag Weak.
Bob and Weave When you Guard the enemy rolls two attack dice and takes the lowest one.
Adrenaline Rush Each turn in combat you get +1 to melee attacks.
Sisu You can take an additional critical hit before you die.

Ranged Weaponry

Marksmanship Ranged attacks have Precise.
Weapon Toss You throw your weapon at the enemy. Add the tags Ranged, Deadly, Stunning. Requires a blunt weapon.
Helmsplitter You bow attacks gain +1 intensity with Headshots.
Run and Gun You may attack with a ranged weapon even if you have been attacked in melee this round.
Gunslinger If you have a pistol equipped at the start of combat you may shoot one enemy in sight for free.
Sniper Gain Draw a Bead.
Draw a Bead While you are out of combat you can take a prone position to increase your accuracy with rifles. When you're ready you take the shot, dealing kill damage to any location on the target. Once fired the enemy knows you are here.
Boneshaker Killing attacks with Shotguns count as 2 Kills for the sake of armor and block. (4 critical wounds).

Medical Studies

Medical Intervention You can prevent death rolls by working on an ally suffering from critical wounds. Patch Wounds heals Major Damage instead of Minor Damage.
Analyze As an action choose an enemy and a body part. All allies get +1 intensity for attacking that specific spot once.
Organ Harvest/Medicinal You can harvest the origins of the recently deceased. Gain Medicinal.
Medicinal You may sacrifice an organ to cure ailments for you or other characters:
  • Liver (Poisoned)
  • Kidney (Nausea)
  • Eye (Blindness)
  • Heart (Bleeding)
  • Parathyroid glands (Fracture)
  • Thymus (Infection)
Magna-Medicinal Sacrifice a limb to restore someone from death. Remove a limb to heal a recently deceased character to 1 Major wound.
Psychology Gain Therapy.
Therapy During Downtime when you have time and emotional intimacy with a PC or NPC you can read them more deeply than normal.
Roll+Bookish or Roll+Persuasive. On a 10+ choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1:
  • What was the character's lowest moment?
  • For what does the character crave forgiveness, and of whom?
  • What are the character's secret pains?
  • In what ways are the character's mind and soul vulnerable?
On a miss, choose 1, but the DM makes a soft move.
Either way the character receives 20 Mind

Magic

Bury the Trauma Take half Mind damage (round down). Does not apply to spells or abilities with a Mind cost.
Meditation At the start of Downtime you regain 10 Mind.
Occultism Gain an additional Rev Token at the start of combat.
Blood Sacrifice You may inflict a Major wound on yourself to cast a spell instead of a spell with a Mind cost.
La Danse Macabre You do the ritualistic dance of the Yellow Mages enhancing your magical power. You gain +1 to your spells every turn of combat.
Engraving Carve the rune of an Old God on your flesh to gain their power. You may only carve one rune and you need at least 1 Affinity with the Old God.
  • Gro-Goroth: +1 Occult (Max 3)
  • Sylvian: +1 Persuasive (Max 3)
  • Vinushka: +1 Bookish (Max 3)
  • Rher: +25 Max Mind (Max 150)
  • Astrea: +1 Clever (Max 3)
  • All-mer: +1 Sturdy (Max 3)
Advanced Occultism Gain a Rev Token at the start of each round of combat.
Counter Magic You may dispel magical runes or other magical effects out of combat. Magic attacks against you roll twice and take the lower result.
Spice Forge During Downtime you may enhance three of your spells with powerful Eastern Sanctuary spices. Each spell can only have one enhancement.
  • First: You may cast this spell as soon as combat starts before any character takes an action.
  • Double: When you cast this spell you cast it twice. Each cast takes Mind cost.
  • Greater: You reduce the Mind cost of the spell by â…“ (round down).

Survival

Survivalist You always have a 10+ on Scrounge for Food.
Resourceful All Downtime Moves cost 1 less point.
Fast Learner Once per Downtime you may use any Downtime Move your party members have. You may repeat a Downtime move.
Devour Can eat the corpses for bonuses. The effect lasts until the next downtime.
  • Human - Next Downtime you do not need to eat.
  • Beast - +1 to all melee attacks.
  • Construct - +1 natural armor.
  • Wizard - +1 to all spells.
  • Horror - +1 to all rolls.
Coolheaded When making a Pressure roll replace the bonus of the stat with +3.

Herbalism

Botany During downtime you can forage for herbs and helpful plants. You find 1+Bookish Herbs.
Expert Botany Gain Undergrowth Awareness.
Undergrowth Awareness Gather advanced herbs and plants used for specific alchemic recipes. You can gather 1+Bookish of the following:
  • Golden Chantell
  • Fly Antima
  • Hemlock
  • Nettle
Toxicology During Downtime you can create potent poisons.
  • Concentrated Hemlock (2 Hemlock + 1 Herb)
  • Concentrated Nettle (2 Nettle + 1 Herb)
You may also add the Tag: Poison to your weapon.
Basic Alchemy During Downtime you can create potions and tinctures from Herbs. Each costs 2 Herbs.
  • Blue Vial
  • Red Vial
  • Antidote
  • Drugs
Advanced Alchemy During Downtime you can create advanced potions.
  • Light Blue Vial (4 Herbs)
  • Stimulant (Golden Chantell + Blue Vial)
  • Adrenaline Potion (Golden Chantell + Fly Antima)
  • Psychedelics (Fly Antima + Drugs)

Tinkering

Trapcraft During Downtime you can create traps. Each trap costs 2 Scrap.
  • Molotov Cocktail
  • Shrapnel Grenade
  • Bear Trap
Weaponcraft During Downtime you can augment a weapon. Choose a weapon and give it a new tag. Tags do not stack.
  • Sharpened - Tag: Bleeding
  • Extended - Tag: Long
  • Honed - Tag: Deadly
  • Forceful - Tag: Stunning
  • Balanced - Tag: Versatile
  • Accurate - Tag: Precise
  • Light - Tag: Quick
Find Scrap During downtime you can search for mechanical Scrap. You find 1+Clever Scrap.
Armorer Mend Armor costs 2 less points and 1 less Scrap.
Explosives During Downtime you can create explosives. Cool. Each costs 4 Scrap.
  • Landmine
  • Pipe Bomb

Other Skills

Adaptability You can always take adaptability. You can only take adaptability once. Choose a basic ability (abilities in the inner ring of the hexen). After the next downtime gain that ability.
