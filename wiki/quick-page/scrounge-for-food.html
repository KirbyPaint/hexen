Scrounge For Food

Roll+Clever. On a 10+ you find 1 Food. On a 7-9 you find 1 Food and pick 1 from the options below:

  • The food is spoiled. (The Food causes Nausea when consumed)
  • You have to fight for it. (Take a minor wound)
  • You stole it from someone or something in need. (Take 5 Mind Damage)

On a miss you find 1 food, but prepare for the worst.
