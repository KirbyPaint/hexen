Character Sheet

Name: The name of those who will suffer.

Soul: The soul given to the character at birth. Roleplaying only.

Profession: General description of what they did before the terrible events to follow.

Phobia: What they are truly scared of. You will incur minuses when interacting/fighting these creatures.

Body: Health and Stamina

Your body is divided into six parts:

  • Head
  • Body
  • Left Arm
  • Right Arm
  • Left Leg
  • Right Leg

Each limb and the head can take 4 ticks of damage. The body can take 6 ticks of damage. Minor damage is one box, major damage is two boxes, and critical is 4 boxes. You cannot go past the max number of boxes. No overkill.

If you have two critical wounds on your limbs (four boxes ticked on one limb) or one critical wound on your head or body, then your character dies.

Minor wound - scratches, scuffs, black eye. Will heal on its own.

Major wound - part is hurt. Still usable but requires medical attention.

Critical wound - part is unusable. Major medical attention is needed or the character will die.

Mind: Sanity and spell resources.

Your mind is your current mental state on how you are dealing with the horrors of the world. This will go down with Fear rolls. Most spells also take Mind to use.

  • Sane - 100-51
  • Afraid - 50-26
  • Panicked - 25-1
  • Psychotic break - 0 (Not good)

If you are at 0 Mind and take mental damage, flip a coin and call a side. If you are right you take 1 critical damage to one of your limbs. If you are wrong your character dies.

You can regain Mind when you rest. There are other ways to restore it, but they are not pretty...

Stats: How your character is defined.

  • Sturdy - Your physicality and how strong your willpower is.
  • Clever - You wits and how resourceful you are.
  • Bookish - Your intellect and how well read you are.
  • Persuasive - Your charisma and you well you can connect to others.
  • Occult - Your knowledge and how connected you are to the magic of this world.

At character creation you place 2, 1, 0, 0,-1, in stats as you wish.

Weapons: The name of the weapon you are using any all tags associated with it.

Armor: The armor you are wearing and how many uses it still has left.

Items: A list of what you have on your person.

Abilities: A list of what your abilities are and what they do.

Affinity: How devout to the Old Gods you are. Raise this to gain their blessings and powers.

Connection: How you relate to the other characters. At character creation you place 0, 0, -1 as you wish.
